India's highest WaterFall

Jog Falls is a waterfall on the Sharavati river located in Siddapur taluk of Uttara Kannada district and its view point in Kargal town of Shimoga district, Karnataka, India.

Journey to Woods

Dandeli is a fascinating and adventurous city situated on the Western Ghats in Uttarakannada popular for its lush dense deciduous forests, deep valleys, wildlife sanctuaries, bird watching, water sports, river side fishing, trekking on hilly terrains, mountain biking, night camps and scenic beauty.

A coastal beauty

Honnavar is a charming coastal town located in the Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India. Nestled between the Arabian Sea and the banks of the Sharavathi River, Honnavar boasts a rich history, scenic beauty, and a laid-back atmosphere.
